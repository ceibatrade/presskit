About the Ceiba International Group Journey

We are dedicated to delivering quality products at a fair price to our clients around the world. We are pleased to offer a wide range of products. If you are looking for 100% organic fruits and vegatables, farm raised seafood, or sustainably sourced materials, give us a call and find out how we can help you!

Sustainable Sourcing is the integration of social, ethical and environmental performance factors into the process of selecting suppliers. One of the ultimate goals of Ceiba International Group is to build strong, long-term relationships with our suppliers.

Importers /Exporters of Fine Sustainable Products

Sustainable products are products that provide many environmental, social and economic benefits.Sustainability is protecting the public’s health and the environment while extracting raw materials

At Ceiba International Group we care about our environmental impact and the local markets from which we receive our products. We believe in reinvestment in these communities. Giving back by providing storage facilities to farmers so that their products aren’t lost to spoilage and constructing irrigation systems. Working with local provincial governments to help implement and improved agricultural practices. Helping to create a sustainable commercial market for the local communities and ensures the highest quality products for our customers.

Driven to connect communities with 100% organic and sustainable sourced products from across the globe.
